About Us

Redefining Talent Acquisition

Welcome to Findix Private Limited, where we are redefining the way talent meets opportunity. At Findix Private Limited, we understand the pivotal role that the right talent plays in driving organizational success. That's why we have embarked on a mission to connect top-tier talent with innovative companies, fostering growth and prosperity on both sides.


Founded by a team of industry veterans, Findix Private Limited brings a fresh approach to recruitment,blending cutting-edge technology with a personalized touch. Our platform harnesses the power of AI-driven matchmaking algorithms to streamline the hiring process, ensuring efficient, precise, and tailored matches.


We are more than just a platform; we are your strategic partner in talent acquisition. Our team of dedicated professionals is committed to understanding your unique needs and culture, enabling us to deliver candidates who not only meet the skill requirements but also align with your organization's values and vision.

◎ Our Mission

"Empowering organizations by connecting them with exceptional talent to drive innovation and growth."
At Findix, we believe that great talent is the foundation of successful organizations. Our mission is to bridge the gap between employers and qualified professionals, creating meaningful connections that foster innovation, drive growth, and shape the future of work.

◎ Our Vision

"Transforming the future of work through seamless, data-driven talent connections." We envision a world where talent acquisition is no longer a challenge but an opportunity for growth. By leveraging technology and human expertise, we aim to revolutionize how organizations build teams, making the process more efficient, effective, and enjoyable for all stakeholders involved.

Our Services

Findix Pvt Ltd is a recruitment solutions provider driven by innovation and efficiency. We're passionate about connecting businesses with top-tier talent through a seamless, data-driven approach that removes the guesswork and accelerates the hiring journey.

Staffing & Recruitment

Sourcing, screening, & hiring qualified candidates to fill job roles within an organization. The process ensures a businesses have the right talent to meet their operational and strategic goals.

Executive Search

Identifying & hiring top-level executives and senior leadership roles.

Contract Staffing

Hiring employees on temporary basis for specific project or the duration, providing flexibility & cost-effectiveness for businesses.

Permanent Placement

Recruiting & hiring full-time employees who become long-term assets to an organization.

RPO (Recruitment Process Outsourcing)

We do strategic partnership to manages a company's recruitment process to improve efficiency and cost effective on monthly basis cost.

Manpower (Skilled / Unskilled)

Provides skilled and unskilled workers to meet workforce demands across various industries, includes sourcing, screening, arranging interview and onboarding.
